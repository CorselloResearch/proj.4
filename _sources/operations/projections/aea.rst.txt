********************************************************************************
Albers Equal Area
********************************************************************************
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Classification**  | Conic.                                                   |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Available forms** | Forward and inverse, spherical and elliptical projection.|
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Defined area**    | Global.                                                  |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| **Options**                                                                    |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| `+lat_1`            | First standard parallel. Defaults to 0.0.                |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| `+lat_2`            | Second standard parallel. Can not be equal to `lat_1`.   |
|                     | Defaults to 0.0.                                         |
+---------------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
